About This Archive

This site preserves historical versions of New Zealand government procurement guidance documents that are no longer available through official channels. These archived materials serve as a resource for researchers, academics, and procurement professionals requiring access to previous versions of procurement frameworks.

Current versions of all documents link directly to their authoritative sources on government websites. Historical versions are provided as downloadable PDF files.
